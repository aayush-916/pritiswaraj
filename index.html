Book Publication

A History of Buddhist Iconography in Bihar

This book presents a comprehensive study on Buddhist iconography in Bihar, examining the rich sculptural traditions, their philosophical context, and Indo‑Tibetan connections. It draws upon field research, museum collections, and historical sources to provide insights into the evolution of Buddhist art and iconography in the region.

About

Education & Training

Dr. Priti Swaraj completed M.A. in History and earned a Ph.D. from Nava Nālandā Mahavihara (Deemed University). She has taken specialist courses including Tibetan language (Tibet House, Delhi), Rock Art of Southeast Asia (SAMEO‑SPAFA, Thailand) and Introduction to Archaeology (Bihar Museum).
